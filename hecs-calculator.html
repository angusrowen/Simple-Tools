From 2025–26, HECS repayments use a marginal rate system — you only pay the rate on income above each threshold, not on your entire income.

Repayment IncomeRate / Calculation
$0 – $67,000Nil
$67,001 – $125,00015c per $1 over $67,000
$125,001 – $179,285$8,700 + 17c per $1 over $125,000
$179,286 and above10% of total repayment income

Indexation is applied on 1 June each year at the lower of CPI or WPI. The 2025 rate is 3.2%.
